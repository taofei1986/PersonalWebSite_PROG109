Handsome Cat

Cat from hell

A ghost, though invisible, still is like a place your sight can knock on, echoing; but here within this thick black pelt, your strongest gaze will be absorbed and utterly disappear:

just as a raving madman, when nothing else can ease him, charges into his dark night howling, pounds on the padded wall, and feels the rage being taken in and pacified.

A Man on Bench

Reading with sunshine

There's a guy sitting on a park bench reading a newspaper. I wonder how long he's been there. I really wish I knew what he was doing because it seems like he's been there a long time. The headline on the front page read "Thousands Flee in Panic!" I wondered what thousands were fleeing in panic from and I tried to get a closer look at the paper. It had a photo of a huge fire at the landfill, and it looked like gulls flying away. Then my eye caught the subtitle... "Thousands of Gulls Flee Dump of Fiery Inferno."
